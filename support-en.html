Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I connect with my partner?

Go to Settings > Partner Management to generate an invite code. Your partner enters the code to connect automatically.

Q. Is my data secure?

All data is encrypted via TLS/HTTPS and stored securely on our servers. Only connected partners can view the shared ledger.

Q. How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account Management > Delete Account. All data will be permanently removed upon deletion.

Q. Can I use it alone?

Yes, you can use Solo mode as a personal budget tracker. You can connect a partner later to switch to Couple mode.

Q. What languages are supported?

We support Korean and English by default, as well as Japanese, Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), and Indonesian.
